🔬Calculation Methods
  • BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor): Males: 10×W + 6.25×H − 5×A + 5 | Females: 10×W + 6.25×H − 5×A − 161
  • TDEE: BMR × Activity Factor
  • Protein Target: 0.8–1.6 g/kg body weight depending on activity
  • Micronutrient RDAs: Based on ICMR 2020 (Indian adult, 19–50 yr)
Data Accuracy Note Food composition values are approximate averages from USDA/NIN databases. Actual nutrient content varies by brand, cooking method, and ingredient quality. Values for raw/uncooked ingredients are used unless stated.
